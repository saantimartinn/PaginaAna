OUR PRODUCT

Tumor cells frequently suppress anti-tumor immunity through PD-1/PD-L1 signaling, leading to T-cell exhaustion and reduced cytotoxic activity.

Our therapeutic strategy is designed to interfere with this pathway while simultaneously enhancing local immune activation within the tumor microenvironment.

The intended outcome is a dual-mechanism immunotherapy capable of restoring T-cell function, promoting local immune infiltration, and potentially reducing systemic toxicity.

THERAPEUTIC INDICATIONS

  • Head and neck squamous cell carcinoma (HNSCC): Suitable for therapies that block immune checkpoints and enhance cytokine signaling.
  • Malignant pleural mesothelioma (MPM): Targeted cytokine-checkpoint fusion may convert a “cold” microenvironment into an immune-responsive state.
